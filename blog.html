Keep On Moving

Title description, December 9, 2014

Take Another Step It is said that great writers don’t take days off. Many people let creativity come to them and on days when they don’t feel it, they take a day off. But sometimes one day can lead to two days and so forth. Everyday you must be building good habits. Therefore, like all things, such as faith, habits, and discipline, I will continue to motivate myself to be better. I won’t take a step back. But like that quote, “Sometimes you have to take one step back to take two steps forward”, right when I take that first step back, I will immediately take two steps forward.
